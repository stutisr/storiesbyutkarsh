MEET UTKARSH

"..And there I sat, by the endless sea..music rushing through my veins like the sea breeze"

Hello there! I am a singer-songwriter from the northern plains of India. I've been playing the guitar for about 11 years now. I play the percussive fingerstyle acoustic guitar. I write songs about social changes, humanity and inner battles. I quit my job and got into music full time very recently to be more involved in the art and utilise this potential for bigger things that matter. This is my portal where I connect with the world and share all that I have learnt and created over the years. I hope you are enchanted! Cheers!
