MEET UTKARSH

"..And there I sat, by the endless sea..music rushing through my veins like the sea breeze"

Every song has a story to tell. A self taught musician, from the northern plains of India, Utkarsh has been inspired by this belief to create music since the last ten years. Known for his unique style of playing the guitar, Utkarsh believes in making music that transcends genres keeping it unconventional yet original.

After getting a degree in engineering and working a corporate job for two years, he learnt that his true passion was calling out to him. He left the comforts of a regualr life and immersed himself into music. Ever since, he has been composing pieces of music by himself as well as collaborating with different artists from around the world. Featured extensively on various music platforms, his music is well received by people everywhere.

Utkarsh plans to continue making music for the rest of his life and bring more smiles into this world. This is his space to connect with the world and showcase his work.
